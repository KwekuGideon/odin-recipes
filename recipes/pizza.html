Vegetable Pizza

Pizza is a popular dish consisting of a dough base topped with sauce, cheese, and various ingredients. Originating from Italy, it has become a stable food worldwide, known for its versatility, ease of preparation, and delicious taste. Its popularity has grown with new variations and toppings, and its appeal continues to endure.

Ingredients

Crust:

  • All-purpose flour
  • Active dry yeast
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Olive oil

Toppings:

  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Your choice of vegetables, such as:
    • Bell peppers
    • Onions
    • Mushrooms
    • Olives
    • Spinach
    • Artichokes
    • Zucchini
    • Eggplant
    • Broccoli
    • Tomatoes
    • Caramelized onions

Seasonings(optional):

  • Dried basil
  • Dried oregano
  • Garlic powder
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Note: You can adjust the amount of each ingredient based on your preference.

Steps

Here is a summarized list on how to prepare the vegetable pizza with tomato:

  • Make the dough:Mix flour, yeast, sugar, salt, water, and oil. Knead, rise, and roll out the dough. Place it on a baking sheet.
  • Add the toppings: Spread tomato sauce, sprinkle cheese, and add vegetables. Season with basil, oregano, garlic, red pepper, salt, and pepper.
  • Bake: Bake the pizza in a preheated oven for 15-20 minutes.
  • Serve and enjoy!
